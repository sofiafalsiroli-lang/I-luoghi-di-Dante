Welcome

The website “Sulle Tracce di Dante Alighieri” was born from the interest in the figure of the Italian "Sommo Poeta" and his relationship with the Italian territory. Its aim is to offer an overview—though not exhaustive—of the main Italian cities and regions where Dante stayed during his exile (1302–1321), highlighting for each of them places of interest (museums, plaques, statues…) connected to his life and legacy.

To explore the different locations, users can either use the interactive map (in the “map” section) or access the dropdown menu in the information bar and select the desired place.

I hope that this platform may serve as a source of inspiration to follow in the footsteps of the “Ghibellin fuggiasco” and open up new perspectives on the Italian landscape.

For more detailed information about the development of the website and the sources used, please visit the "About the Website" page.
